Biography

Dembélé started his career at Madeleine Évreux at the age of 7. He then spent 5 years at the club before moving to Évreux. He later moved to Évreux FC 27 between the ages of 12 and 13. Rennes brought him to their youth setup a year later. Dembélé had to wait for 4 years to start his club career. He first featured for Rennes's reserve side as a 78th-minute substitute in the Championnat de France Amateur, on 6th September 2014. On 6th September 2015, Dembélé made his professional debut for Rennes' first team in Ligue 1 against Angers and came off the bench in the last 5 minutes. On 12th May 2016, Dembélé signed a five-year contract with German club Borussia Dortmund in 2016. On 14th August 2016, he made his debut in 2–0 defeat to Bayern Munich in the DFL-Supercup. In August 2017, he signed for Barcelona. Dembele made his debut for the Spanish club a month later on the 9th of September, 2017. For France, He made his debut on 1st September, 2016 against Italy at the Stadio San Nicola. He was also part of the World Cup winning French squad in 2018.

Jordi Alba Ramos is a Spanish professional footballer was born on 21st of March 1989 in L'Hospitalet, Spain. He has the swiftest shoes on the field which enables him to cover the ground in a very short time. Jordi generally plays as a left back and at times as a left winger. He carries a jersey number 18 and currently plays for Spanish giant club Barcelona. His youth career started with Barcelona in the year 1997. However, he was only 8 years old and was considered to be very young and was released by the club in 2005. He completed his football education at Valencia in 2008, after joining Cornella for two years.

Memphis Depay is a Dutch professional footballer who was born on 13 February 1994 in Moordrecht, Netherlands.Memphis is two-footed and a winger who plays mainly in the position of left-wing. Currently, he is serving Barcelona FC and Netherlands national team. He is widely considered as one of the best young players in the world football. He was also given the Best Young Player in the world award by France Football in 2015. Memphis playing style is very much similar to Cristiano Ronaldo, as a result, he is often compared to him. Ronaldo himself praised him saying, “Memphis is one of the best young players in the world.” Memphis began his career at the age of 6 by joining local academic club of his hometown, Moordrecht. Staying with the club for three straight years, he was trained and cultivated. In 2003, he united with Eredivisie club Sparta Rotterdam and played for the club till 2006. Eventually, he moved to PSV Eindhoven and played for the youth team of the club until he got promoted to the senior team in 2011. Memphis has also represented Netherlands at all the international levels including U-17, U-19 and U-21.
